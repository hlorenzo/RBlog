Biography

Hadrien Lorenzo is a PhD student in the SISTM team. He mainly works on analysing high dimensional datasets (just a few individuals and thousands of variables) with longitudinal structures. SVD-based methods are what he loves the most and he currently works on dealing with missing values in supervised context.

SISTM is mainly devoted to vaccine research and clinical trial data analyses in the context of largely scaled datasets. This is on of the few INSERM/INRIA teams.
